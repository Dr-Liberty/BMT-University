Project Overview

The Problem

Crypto projects struggle to onboard users who understand their technology. Traditional education doesn't incentivize completion, resulting in 90%+ dropout rates. Meanwhile, users want to earn while learning but face scams and low-quality content.

Our Solution: BMT University

A fully-functional learn-to-earn platform where users complete crypto courses and earn $BMT token rewards instantly. Built specifically for the Kaspa ecosystem, leveraging Kasplex L2 for smart contract functionality and testing on Igra Network.

  • Complete interactive lessons with embedded quizzes
  • Earn $BMT tokens immediately upon course completion
  • Wallet-based authentication (no passwords, no emails)
  • Anti-abuse security system with velocity limits and cluster detection
